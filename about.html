Our Mission

Our mission is to empower women in Rwanda by providing them with the resources and support they need to produce high-quality coffee and earn a sustainable income. We believe that by investing in women, we can create a brighter future for our community and our country.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading digital producer of specialty coffee in Rwanda by 2024 while promoting gender equality and sustainable development. We aim to create a model for other cooperatives to follow and inspire positive change in our industry and beyond.
